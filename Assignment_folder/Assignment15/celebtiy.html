Biography

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, is a prominent Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. Known for his aggressive batting style and exceptional leadership skills, Kohli has established himself as one of the best batsmen in the world.

Achievements

  • Most centuries in run chases in ODIs.
  • Fastest player to reach 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000, and 12,000 runs in ODIs.
  • ICC Cricketer of the Year: 2017, 2018.
  • Many more records and accolades...
